About SEBET

IS the Rabbit, LLC and our app, SEBET, grew out of more than a quarter century experience in the commercial and consumer banking industries. We saw community banks bought up by behemoths, and most of these types of companies, either by design or forced by competition, have one aim, to squeeze as much of profit as they can out of you, your family and your business. We formed our initial app, SEBET to help people keep more of their money, and to pay themselves, instead of paying the bank or credit card company.

01

Zero-based Budget

Zero-based goal with SEBET is to organize the actual spending of your income into the goals that you set for yourself.

02

Sinking Funds

Enter an amount for your goal, and when you need to be that goal, then SEBET will let you know how much you need to budget each month to reach it!

03

Debt Snowball

Enter your debts here. Add something extra that you want to throw at these debts if you can and press the button to begin your debt snowball plan!


04

Mileage Tracking

Enter your work schedule. Then as the month goes on, anytime your phone goes with you in a car when you are working, SEBET will track your mileage for you!

05

Expense Report

SEBET will send you automatically generated reports on the email address where you want automated reports to go. You, or your accountant’s; it’s your choice.
